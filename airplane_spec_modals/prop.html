Propeller

We use a Sensenich ground-adjustable propeller for this airplane. Deciding against using a constant-speed propeller for the purposes of reduced maintenance and cost, the ground-adjustable propeller provides an awesome alternative to tuning the airplane's performance right where we want it.

For the RV-10 build, we will likely be using a constant-speed propeller. One of the biggest downsides to not having a constant-speed propeller is being able to slow down as easily when coming in to land.
